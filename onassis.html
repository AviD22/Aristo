Aristotle Onassis


1906 - 1975


One of the richest people in 20th century


Aristotle Socrates Onassis (/oʊˈnæsɪs/, US also /-ˈnɑː-/;[1] Greek: Αριστοτέλης Ωνάσης, romanized: Aristotélis Onásis, pronounced [aristoˈtelis oˈnasis]; 20 January 1906 – 15 March 1975),[2] was a Greek[3][4] shipping magnate who amassed the world's largest privately-owned shipping fleet and was one of the world's richest men.



    The following list is a timeline of Onassis Life.
  • 1906 - born in Karataş, a suburb of the port city of Smyrna.
  • 1922 - He left Smyrna during the great fire of Smyrna in 1922.
  • 1932 - He went to Buenos Aires, Argentina and start working as telephone operator.
  • 1939 - He relocated to New York and started his shipping businesses.
  • 1946 - Onassis married Athina Livanos.
  • 1968 - Onassis announced the launch of Project Omega.
  • 1975 - Onassis died at the age of 69, on 15th March 1975.
